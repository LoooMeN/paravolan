PARAVOLAN is one of the leading evacuation organizations in Ukraine, driven by excellence in its mission.

Our team of dedicated professionals excels in systematically executing mass evacuations of the wounded, operating in close coordination with combat medics of the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

FIVE YEARS OF SYSTEMATIC EVACUATIONS

Since 2020, our team has been evacuating seriously ill children and adults from abroad and across Ukraine. In 2021, and before the start of a full-scale invasion in coordination with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, dozens of seriously ill Ukrainians were evacuated from the Republic of Belarus. From February 24, 2022, the team took on the military evacuation of wounded soldiers from stabilization points and front-line hospitals.

COMPLETELY INDEPENDENT WORK OF THE ORGANIZATION

The Paravolan team consists of drivers and medics who are professionals in their field and are Ukrainian patriots. Most of the people have worked in the organization since the very beginning of the mission. We have a powerful vehicle fleet, which includes a refueling station, as well as more than fifteen units of transport for the simultaneous evacuation of more than 200 defenders. The transport has the necessary medical equipment and places for the seriously injured.
